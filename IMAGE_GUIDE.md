## Image Optimization Tips

For best website performance:
- Resize images to appropriate dimensions (max 1920px wide for hero, 800px for cards)
- Compress images (use TinyPNG, ImageOptim, or similar)
- Use modern formats (WebP with JPG fallback)
- Add descriptive alt text for accessibility and SEO

## Current Image Specifications

All images currently use:
```css
style="width: 100%; height: 250px; object-fit: cover; border-radius: 8px;"
```

- **object-fit: cover** ensures images fill the space without distortion
- **border-radius: 8px** gives rounded corners
- **height: 250px** for service cards, **200px** for equipment cards
