How to Change Colors

  1. Open css/styles.css
  2. Find lines 4-5 (the two main color variables)
  3. Change --color-primary and --color-secondary to your hex colors
  4. Save and refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5)
  5. The entire website updates instantly!

See COLOR_SYSTEM_GUIDE.md for example color schemes and detailed instructions.
