Quality & Capabilities

Quality products and services are the foundation for customer satisfaction

We deliver parts right the first time. Our commitment to quality is reflected in continued investment in the latest QA equipment and rigorous inspection processes. Every part is inspected to meet your exact specifications, ensuring consistent results and on-time delivery.

In-Process Inspection

Quality checks during production ensure problems are caught early, reducing waste and maintaining consistency throughout the manufacturing process.

Advanced Inspection Equipment

State-of-the-art measuring tools including optical gauging, CMM, and laser scanning for accurate verification of tight tolerances.

Regular Calibration

All QA equipment and machines are regularly calibrated internally and by outside calibration services to maintain accuracy.

Documentation & Traceability

Complete documentation of processes, materials, and inspections for full traceability and quality records.

Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM)

High-precision 3D measurement for complex geometries and tight tolerances. Provides detailed dimensional analysis and verification.

Optical Gauging Machine

Non-contact optical measurement system for quick, accurate inspection of profiles, edges, and features without part deformation.

FARO Arm (10' Reach)

Portable coordinate measuring arm for large parts and on-machine verification. Ideal for complex components and reverse engineering.

Laser Scanner

Vertek laser scanning system for rapid surface measurement and inspection. Perfect for complex 3D shapes and contoured surfaces.

Precision Measuring Tools

Complete set of micrometers, calipers, height gauges, pin gauges, and indicators for manual verification and spot checks.
