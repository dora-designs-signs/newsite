CNC Routing for Electrical Insulation Materials

Specialized equipment for dusty fiberglass, G-10/FR-4, and GPO materials up to 4'×8'

Most machine shops avoid electrical insulation materials due to abrasive tooling wear and hazardous dust. Our CNC router is specifically configured with dust collection systems, specialized tooling, and optimized speeds/feeds for G-10, FR-4, GPO-3, and fiberglass laminates. Large 4'×8' capacity handles switchgear panels, busbar supports, and data center components that won't fit in traditional mills.

Equipment & Capabilities

4'×8' Working Area

Dual spindle configuration with massive work envelope for large panels and multiple part nesting

Automatic Tool Changer

ATC system for efficient multi-tool operations without manual intervention

Vacuum Table System

Secure material holding for thin sheets and flexible materials

8ft Rotary Axis

Cylindrical work capability for tubes, pipes, and round components

Nesting Software

Optimize material usage and minimize waste with intelligent part arrangement

Complex Geometries

3D profiles, cutouts, pocketing, and engraving capabilities

Ideal Applications

  • Large electrical insulation panels
  • Switchgear components and enclosures
  • Custom panels and brackets
  • Signage and displays
  • Gaskets and seals
  • Prototype to production runs
  • Composite and fiberglass parts
  • Wood and acrylic fabrication
