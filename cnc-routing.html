CNC Routing & Machining

Large-scale precision cutting for complex geometries

Our advanced CNC router handles complex geometries on large-scale parts with precision and efficiency. Ideal for dusty composites, fiberglass, plastics, and electrical insulation materials that aren't suited for traditional machining centers.

Equipment & Capabilities

4'×8' Working Area

Dual spindle configuration with massive work envelope for large panels and multiple part nesting

Automatic Tool Changer

ATC system for efficient multi-tool operations without manual intervention

Vacuum Table System

Secure material holding for thin sheets and flexible materials

8ft Rotary Axis

Cylindrical work capability for tubes, pipes, and round components

Nesting Software

Optimize material usage and minimize waste with intelligent part arrangement

Complex Geometries

3D profiles, cutouts, pocketing, and engraving capabilities

  • Switchgear components and enclosures
  • Custom panels and brackets
  • Signage and displays
  • Gaskets and seals
  • Prototype to production runs
