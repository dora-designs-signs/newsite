CNC Routing

Advanced CNC router handling complex geometries on large-scale parts. Ideal for dusty composites, fiberglass, plastics, and electrical insulation materials that aren't suited for traditional machining centers.

Capabilities

  • 4'x8' working area with dual spindle configuration
  • Automatic Tool Changer (ATC) for efficient operations
  • Vacuum table for secure material holding
  • 8ft rotary axis for cylindrical parts
  • Nesting capability to reduce material waste and costs
  • Complex profiles, cutouts, and engraving

Ideal For

  • Large electrical insulation panels
  • Switchgear components
  • Custom enclosures and panels
  • Prototype to production runs
