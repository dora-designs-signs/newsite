About Us

Precision manufacturing with a commitment to quality and innovation

Electrical Insulation Specialists in Mount Dora, Florida

Located in Mount Dora, Florida (greater Orlando area), CFL Machine serves switchgear manufacturers, data centers, and power distribution equipment suppliers nationwide with precision electrical insulation components.

Our Specialty: Materials Others Avoid

Since 1985, we've specialized in machining electrical insulation materials that most shops refuse. G-10/FR-4, GPO-3, and fiberglass laminates are extremely abrasive on tooling and generate hazardous dust‚Äîbut these challenging materials are our expertise.

We've invested in specialized dust collection systems, premium carbide tooling, and decades of process refinement to become the go-to shop for electrical insulation components. Whether you need busbar supports, arc flash barriers, terminal boards, or custom switchgear insulation‚Äîwe deliver precision parts on time.

About the Owner

Owner/operator Jon Waldmann started Waldmann Enterprises in 2020, bringing a BS in Mechanical Engineering from UCF and 7 years of medical product design and CNC manufacturing experience. His background in precision manufacturing and engineering problem-solving drives our commitment to quality.

Jon's hands-on approach and technical expertise ensure every electrical insulation component meets strict tolerances. From CAD design support to final inspection, we understand the critical nature of electrical components for data centers, switchgear, and power distribution systems.
