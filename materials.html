Electrical Insulation Materials

Decades of experience machining abrasive fiberglass laminates and NEMA-grade materials

We specialize in materials that challenge most machine shops. G-10/FR-4, GPO-3, and fiberglass laminates are extremely abrasive and generate hazardous dust—but these are exactly the materials we've mastered since 1985. Whether you need switchgear insulation, busbar supports, or data center components, we have the expertise and specialized equipment to machine electrical insulation materials to tight tolerances.
