Electrical Insulation Components

Expert machining of G-10/FR-4, GPO-3, and NEMA-grade materials for critical electrical systems

Machining electrical insulation materials has been our specialty since 1985. Materials used for electrical insulation are extremely abrasive on tooling and generate hazardous dust, but we've refined our processes over decades to become industry experts. Serving switchgear manufacturers, data centers, transformer builders, and power distribution equipment suppliers nationwide.

Materials We Specialize In

Glass Polyester Laminates

  • GPO-1 - General purpose glass polyester for electrical applications
  • GPO-2 - Higher impact resistance, excellent machinability
  • GPO-3 - Superior arc resistance and tracking resistance for high-voltage switchgear
  • Glastic & Haysite - Premium brands for critical applications

Glass Epoxy Laminates (NEMA Grades)

  • G-10/FR-4 - Flame retardant glass epoxy, most common for PCB and electrical use
  • G-11 - High temperature glass epoxy for elevated temperature applications
  • FR-5 - Higher temperature rating than FR-4

Other Glass Fiber Laminates

  • G-5 - Glass melamine, good electrical properties
  • G-7 - Glass silicone, excellent heat resistance
  • G-9 - Glass melamine phenolic

Specialty Electrical Materials

  • Polycarbonate - Lexan, Tuffak for transparent barriers and guards
  • Polypropylene - Formex for lightweight electrical insulation
  • Vulcanized Fiber - Traditional insulation material
  • Phenolic Laminates - Various NEMA grades

Products We Supply

  • Switchgear Components - Barriers, dividers, arc chutes
  • Busbar Insulation - Supports, standoffs, terminal boards
  • Data Center Equipment - UPS components, power distribution parts
  • Transformers - Insulation sheets, terminal boards
  • Circuit Breakers - Arc flash barriers, insulation plates
  • Electrical Busways - Support brackets, insulation panels
  • Motors & Drives - Terminal boards, phase barriers
  • Inverters & Power Supplies - Custom insulation components
