Electrical Insulation Components

Specialists in electrical grade materials for data centers and switchgear

We understand the critical nature of electrical components for data centers, switchgear, and industrial equipment. Our expertise in machining electrical insulation materials ensures optimal performance and reliability for mission-critical applications.

Common Applications

  • Busbar insulation and support components
  • Terminal boards and barriers
  • Arc flash protection barriers
  • Switchgear insulation panels
  • Circuit breaker components
  • Transformer insulation parts

Materials We Specialize In

GPO Series

GPO-1, GPO-2, GPO-3 (Glass Polyester) - Excellent electrical properties and arc resistance for high-voltage applications

G-Series Laminates

G-10/FR-4, G-7, G-11 - High mechanical strength and thermal stability for demanding environments

Specialty Materials

Vulcanized Fiber, Phenolic Laminates - Traditional insulation materials for proven performance
